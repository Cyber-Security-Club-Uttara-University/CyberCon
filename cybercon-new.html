About the Event

Welcome to CyberCon 2025

The most comprehensive cybersecurity conference in Bangladesh, bringing together the brightest minds in the industry.

Hosted by Cyber Security Club

CyberCon 2025 is the flagship event of the Cyber Security Club at Uttara University, designed to bring together students, professionals, and industry experts to explore the latest trends, threats, and innovations in cybersecurity.

Our conference features keynote speeches from industry leaders, hands-on workshops, capture-the-flag competitions, networking sessions, and career opportunities in the rapidly evolving field of cybersecurity.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Goals

Mission

To empower the next generation of cybersecurity professionals through knowledge sharing, practical training, and fostering a community of security-conscious individuals dedicated to protecting digital assets.

Vision

To become Bangladesh's leading platform for cybersecurity education and innovation, bridging the gap between academia and industry while creating a safer digital ecosystem for all.

Goals

  • Educate 500+ participants on emerging cyber threats
  • Connect students with industry professionals
  • Provide hands-on cybersecurity training
  • Foster innovation in digital security
  • Build a strong cybersecurity community
